SEP 10 Freedom Project

Context

The "Freedom Project" for SEP10 is a year-long project all about making a website that informs the viewer of the current and future innovations in the topic of my choosing.

Content

For my project, I chose the topic of Finance. In order to make my website about making people more aware of how to manage their money, I used HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. I also chose to independently study Jekyll.
