Turtle Project

Context

The prompt for this project was to create a movie webpage about anything that doesn't currently exist. The title that I settled on was Sea Turtle Man.

Content

Originally, the movie was supposed to be about a turtle protecting a coral reef from a shark, however as I ran out of time and had to use a single day to finish the text for both the movie and the sequel, the battle never actually took place until the sequel, and even then it was only a single sentence. This made the movie seem more like a short story, as it was mainly just listing out the actions of the shark, such as approaching the reef, swimming past it, coming back to the reef, etc.

Reflection

The hardest part of creating the movie was having an unreliable partner. It's really difficult to make a plan with someone who would barely finish half of their responsibilities. A takeaway that I learned from this movie webpage is that if you want to finish projects that involve working with a partner, you either have to have a good partner that could finish all their responsibilities, or you have to do 80% of the work.

Next Steps

If I had more time to work on this project, I would add more text to it, as from what it currently looks like, it's completely unfinished.
